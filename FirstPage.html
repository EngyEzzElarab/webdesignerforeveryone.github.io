WHO WE ARE

Seoudi Investment Group (SIG) started in 1938 as a small, family-run business, with the foundation of Seoudi Grocery. We have since grown, over the past 76 years, to become one of Egypt’s leading corporations, with projects and investments all over the world. Utilizing the talents of over 4,000 professionals, our group ranks amongst the elite in seven sectors such as: Automotive, Agriculture, Real estate, Construction, Retail, Distribution, Food and Beverage as well as Energy. In 1975, The Modern Motors affliation was the frst in a series of prestigious relationships with other global market entities. Through the decades to follow, and with SIG’s eye on international excellence, dedicated and visionary work was meticulously executed that culminated in the worldwide plan SIG launched in 1996 to become a global holding company.

ABOUT THE FOUNDER

Dr. Abdel Moneim Seoudi

Recognized as one of the Egyptian business pioneers, Dr. Abdel Moneim Souedi not only founded the roots of S.I.G, one of the largest business conglomerates in Egypt, he also played a pivotal role in multiple public service organization such as chairing the board of the federation of the Egyptian industries as well as chairing the Egypt Japan Business Council, to mention a few. Dr.Seoudi’s role in strengthening the cooperation between Egypt and Japan was crowned by His Highness The Emperor of Japan honoring him with the Imperial Rising Sun gold and silver star Decoration on May 2008.

OUR PRESIDENT

Eng. Mohamed Seoudi

Appointed as the President of SIG since 2012 with a clear vision of expanding the horizons of the group by investing in new diversified business sectors such as Fashion Retail, FMCG Distribution and Textile Production and Export. Previously Eng. Seoudi assumed different roles within SIG where he succeeded in obtaining international recognition for his role in increasing the efficiency of business performance to reach international standards. Under his management SIG (Modern Motors) was awarded the Nissan Best Customer Satisfaction Performance in the Middle East in 2004 and the Best Sales Performance Operation in the World for Nissan Forklift in 2008.
